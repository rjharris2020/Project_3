Alternative Vechicles. Environmentally Friendly or Not?

Description

Adoption of electric vehicles has only occured on a mass scale in the past decade, and has taken the auto-industry by storm. With emissions data we wanted to see if there was an obvious correlatoin between EVs on the road and overall passenger vehicles emissions.

Because EVs are tied to the grid it is important to get an idea of how overal grid emissions change over time with EV adoption.

Technology advances each year and with each comes a new model of vehicle. We wanted to see how efficent the most popular EVs where that year and how much pollution they would contribute if driven by the average californian.
